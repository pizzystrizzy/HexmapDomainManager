Display — Realms by tier
Select a View tier to collapse all sub-domains up to that level. Each hex shows the color of its ancestor at that tier (or its own color if it has no ancestor at that tier). Optionally pick an Outline tier to draw a thick colored border around the boundaries of domains at a higher tier — useful for seeing duchy boundaries while viewing at the county level.
Grid / Fill sliders
Grid controls hex line opacity. Fill controls color fill opacity. They are independent. Grid lines always draw on top of fills.
Stronghold workflow
Open New Domain, fill details, click Pick on map — form is stashed and restored. City strongholds used for auto-civ (≤8 hex = civilized, ≤12 = borderlands).
